Delivery

Free Delivery (UK Only)

Returns

Please note that personalised items are not eligible for return!

If for any reason you are not happy with your purchase you have 28 days from the date of receiving the goods to return the to us for a full refund of the cost of the product or exchange of the product. Postage costs will not be refunded where an exchange is required through no fault of our own. The goods must be in unused condition with all tags attached.

Faulty Items and Incorrect Orders

If you believe you have received a faulty or damaged product, please contact us immediately for advice on what to do next.
While we do our best to get orders correct, sometimes errors can still occur. If we have sent you an incorrect order please contact us immediately and we can rectify the error as soon as possible.

Cancelations and Alterations

If you wish to cancel or alter an order, please contact us as soon as possible so we can deal with it before the order is dispatched.
If the order has already been dispatched before we are contacted we will have to treat it as a return.
