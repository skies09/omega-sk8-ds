About Us

Omega skate is an up and coming skate shop, dedicated purely to skateboards and skateboarding.
The company has been set up by two skateboarding enthusiasts, who met through skateboard and share a passion for the sport. We believe skateboarding is a true artform and tricks that are being performed by todays skaters are truly mesmerizing. We are looking to equipt people with the tools needed to continue this trend. Tricks are getting bigger and better every day!
