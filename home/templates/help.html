Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a shop I can visit?

Unfortunately all our business is done online at the moment. We are looking to change this in the future.

Can I custom build a skateboard to my specification?

Yes, if you know the sizes of the equipment you need you can custom build a skateboard. Please visit the Complete Setup Guide

Do I need 1 or 2 trucks?

All trucks are sold in pairs, you will only need to add one set of trucks to your basket.

Do I need grip tape?

All decks are gripped before being shipped, free of charge unless stated otherwise. If you would like a specific grip tape to be used or particular to the way the grip tape is applied, please contact us immediately to arrange this via the contact page

Are wheels sold in sets of four?

Yes, all wheels are sold in sets of four unless stated otherwise.

Do all products come with stickers?

Yes we put free stickers in all products shipped as a thank you for shopping with us.
