Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a shop I can visit?

Unfortunately all our business is done online at the moment.

Can I custom build a skateboard to my specification?

Yes, if you know the sizes of the equipment you need you can custom build a skateboard. Please visit the Complete Setup Guide

Do I need 1 or 2 trucks?

All trucks are sold in pairs, you will only need to add one set of trucks to your basket.

Do I need grip tape?

All decks are gripped before being shipped, free of charge. If you would like a specific grip tape to be used or particular to the way the grip tape is applied, please contact us immediatly to arrange this.
